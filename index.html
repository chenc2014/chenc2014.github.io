I am a Lecturer in the Department of Economics at the University of Oklahoma and a Research Fellow at the IZA - Institute of Labor Economics. My primary research areas are labor economics and the economics of education.

My current research reflects two major lines. The first focuses on evaluating labor market policies (e.g., unemployment insurance and the minimum wage). The second is related to examining policies or labor market conditions that affect the process of human capital accumulation (e.g., the relative wages and ability of school teachers and labor demand shocks and children's cognitive development).
